About Project

ERP, or Enterprise Resource Planning software is usually core software for every large business. According to Wikipedia, ERP is the integrated management of main business processes, often in real-time and mediated by software and technology. ERP is usually referred to as a category of business management software—typically a suite of integrated applications—that an organization can use to collect, store, manage and interpret data from many business activities.


In this project, ERP will be based on balanced score card theory, which suggest for a business to keep a balanced between People, Operation, Customers and Finance. In addition, there will be a section for Project management, KPI reports and Admin section for management of Users and Permissions.
