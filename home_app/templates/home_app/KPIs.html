Finance App


This is KPIs Group of Applications, part of Open ERP

You can either choose Balanced score card or KPIs (Key performance indicators) for selected part of your business.

  • Balanced Score Card - this application wil check if you business KPIs are in balance, between People, Operation, Customers and Finance
  • People KPIs - this application will provide with KPIs related to your People management
  • Operation KPIs - this application will provide with KPIs related to your Operation management
  • Customer KPIs - this application will provide with KPIs related to your Custoemrs management
  • Finance KPIs - this application will provide with KPIs related to your Finance management
