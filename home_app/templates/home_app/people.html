People App


This is People Group of Applications, part of Open ERP

You can use below apps to do following tasks:

  • People Data - to manage people information, such as name, address, start date, etc. You can add, edit, delete and view all relavant information about people in your organisation here.
  • Health, Safety & Environment - to manage health and safety of people in your organisaion, your customers contractors and general public. Plus inpact of your business on environment. You can record accidents, inventigation outcome etc, here.
  • Skills, Learning & Development - to manage skills required by your organisation. You can use this app to allocate skills to people, plan learning and developemnt of your people
  • Disciplinary & Grievance - you can use this app to keep record of live disciplinary and grievance and also to archive them which are closed.
